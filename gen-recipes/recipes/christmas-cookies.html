Chef Gen's Christmas Cookies

Description

Indulge in the festive spirit with these delightful Christmas cookies that are sure to enchant your taste buds and spread joy throughout the season. These cookies are a perfect blend of buttery goodness and holiday magic, adorned with festive sprinkles and a touch of seasonal warmth.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Festive sprinkles for decoration

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another large bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the egg, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and nutmeg to the wet ingredients, and mix until well combined.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until a dough forms.
  6. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto baking sheets, leaving space between each cookie.
  7. Decorate the cookies with festive sprinkles.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
  9. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  10. Enjoy these delicious Christmas cookies with your loved ones!
