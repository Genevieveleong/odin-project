Chef Gen's Christmas Brownies

Description

Delight in the whimsical charm of our Christmas Reindeer Brownies, a festive twist on the classic treat that will bring joy to your holiday gatherings. These decadent brownies boast a rich, fudgy texture and are as each brownie is transformed into adorable reindeer faces, complete with antlers and red candy noses. These delightful treats are not only a feast for the taste buds but also a festive and fun addition to your Christmas dessert table, sure to bring smiles to the faces of young and old alike.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • For Chocolate Ganache:
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 8 ounces dark chocolate, finely chopped
  • For Decorating:
  • Pretzels for antlers
  • Red candy noses (e.g., M&M candies)
  • Candy eyes

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and grease a baking pan.
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over low heat. Remove from heat and stir in the sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. In a separate bowl, sift together the cocoa powder, flour, and salt. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  5. While the brownies are baking, prepare the chocolate ganache by heating the cream until it simmers, then pour it over the chopped chocolate. Stir until smooth.
  6. Once the brownies have cooled, spread the chocolate ganache over the top.
  7. Cut the brownies into triangles and decorate each triangle with pretzels as antlers, red candy noses, and candy eyes to create adorable reindeer faces.
  8. Serve and enjoy these festive Christmas Reindeer Brownies!
