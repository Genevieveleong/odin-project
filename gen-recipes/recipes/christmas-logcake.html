Chef Gen's Christmas Log Cake

Description

Indulge in the festive spirit with our enchanting Christmas Log Cake, a delightful treat that captures the essence of the season. This yule log masterpiece features a rich and moist chocolate sponge cake rolled with a luscious layer of velvety chocolate ganache. The exterior is elegantly adorned to resemble a rustic log, complete with intricate chocolate bark and festive decorations. With each slice, you'll savor the harmonious blend of decadent chocolate and a hint of seasonal joy, making this Christmas Log Cake the perfect centerpiece for your holiday celebrations.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • For Chocolate Ganache:
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 8 ounces dark chocolate, finely chopped
  • For Decoration:
  • Chocolate shavings, powdered sugar, and festive decorations

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat the eggs and sugar until light and fluffy. Add the oil, sour cream, and vanilla extract, and mix well.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  5. Spread the batter evenly onto the prepared baking sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes or until the cake springs back when touched.
  6. While the cake is baking, prepare the chocolate ganache by heating the cream until it simmers, then pour it over the chopped chocolate. Stir until smooth and let it cool slightly.
  7. Once the cake has cooled, spread the chocolate ganache evenly over the surface and roll the cake into a log shape.
  8. Decorate the log cake with chocolate shavings, powdered sugar, and festive decorations.
  9. Refrigerate the log cake for at least 2 hours before serving.
  10. Slice and enjoy this festive and delicious holiday treat!
